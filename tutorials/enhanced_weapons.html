Enhanced Weapons and You

How I Learned to Love the ++Nuke

Intro

Enhanced weapons can be very powerful but some of them are utter crap.  This means, like everything else SMR, we're rolling the dice by spending turns on them.  Is it worth it to repeatedly go out and check for enhanced weapons if the bar is nearby?  Probably.  And do you spend turns to check it when it's in another galaxy?  I don't know, probably not, are we Thevian or Creonti?

The point of my rambling is this: through some trial and error, and a bit of reading the game code, some tricks have been found to streamline the hunt for enhanced weapons.  This is current as of game 145.

Important Meaty Details

The tip, in credits, required to unlock information about an enhanced weapon is based on the type of weapon and the enhancement level.  This is the largest meaty bit in this tutorial.  It means that you can spend credits guessing and, once you've narrowed down the amount where the bartender spills the beans, narrow down the type and enhancement to one of a few options.  This is important later in the game, when money is limitless, because it allows you to spend money to save turns.  There is a table below, sorted by credit cost, that can be used to reference the weapon back from the tip cost.

There is a 6 hour timeout on the enhanced weapon events.  If we tip the man at 04:00, that weapon will be available until 10:00.

There is a 5 hour timer on opening new events.  If we tip at 04:00, every person who tips will get the same tip until 09:00.

All bars give the same tip during an event.  If you get info on a ++HHG in the Alskant galaxy, you're going to get the same tip out in Sombrero or Manton.

The events are triggered by tipping the bartender.  If nobody tips any bartenders from 05:00 until 23:00, no events were generated and no enhanced weapons were available.

For each 100 events:

  • 40 will be single plus enhanced for damage
  • 40 will be single plus enhanced for accuracy
  • 20 will be double plus enhanced
